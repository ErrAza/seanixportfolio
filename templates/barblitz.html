Bar Blitz

Bar Blitz is an exciting new mixology game that tests your skills in making cocktails. Host parties in unique locations around Cape Angeles, a city that is renowned for its party culture. Start from humble beginnings and build your fame to get noticed by the top party venues around the city. Throw parties in interesting locations, from a dive bar through to a super yacht.

Work with an array of fun and challenging mixology tools, from chopping knives to blenders to muddlers. Every tool has a series of unique upgrades that can help you to make your parties the talk of the town.

Join the party on IMVU to create your unique avatar in the game and watch as your IMVU friends join you at your parties.
