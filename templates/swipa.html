Swipa

Swipa is a game developed as an entry into a larger competition run by Serious About Games and in partnership with VPUU. The game made it into the top 4, for a chance to win R1 million in funding from the Western Cape Government.

The game was set to address the problem of youth residing in impoverished and unsafe communities that often exhibit a lack of conscious agency and life skills to guide the making of decisions related to their individual and community development

Gameplay was based off the mechanic of Reigns, but positioned in a way to solve a series of outstanding problems local to South Africa.

Due to the target market being difficult to reach and the conditions they reside in, the game was developed to be extremely lightweight, and be able to be updated without formal updates. The ability to update and build/modify the storyline dynamically through means of a simple Google Sheet.

Basic Facebook auth/login functionality was added to aid distribution.
